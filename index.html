We create Healthy and Sustainable Food Habits.

With the help of psychology, we improve the health of children and the future of the planet

Our Approach

The Problem

Many adults (and kids) don't eat enough daily portions of fruits and vegetables, leading to overweight and health problems.

The Solution

An interactive device for children based on psychological principles and gamification, encouraging healthy eating habits through play and family challenges.

Sustainability

MagicFood is committed to a sustainable future by promoting eating habits that not only improve the children's health, but also that of our planet.

The Psychology behind MagicFood

Operant Conditioning

We use positive reinforcements to encourage healthy eating behaviors, rewarding children's healthy choices.

Observational Learning

Children learn by observing their parents and the characters in our stories, creating positive role models to follow.

Token Economy

We offer small rewards for every portion of fruit or vegetables consumed, which can be exchanged for larger rewards.

Gamification

We turn healthy eating into a fun game and a family challenge, making the experience engaging and motivating.
